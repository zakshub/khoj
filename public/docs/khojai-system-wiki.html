KhojAI VPS System Wiki

Live Architecture, Agents, Automation, and Gaps

Prepared from direct VPS inspection of /var/www/khojai. Version 1.1, 31 May 2026.

Executive Summary

KhojAI is already a live VPS project, separate from the local mirror. It runs a public Urdu Next.js website, an AI content pipeline, generated lesson/image outputs, a scheduler, a SQLite database, and WhatsApp teacher code.

Current public site: https://khojai.zuhaib.pro/. Desired consolidated product destination: https://khoj.zuhaib.pro/. Internal control tower remains https://lab.zuhaib.pro/khoj.

What This Means for Khoj

The existing khojai.zuhaib.pro project is closer to the product experience you want on khoj.zuhaib.pro than the current executor page. It already has the real school front end, latest lessons, generated images, age groups, learning cycle, WhatsApp CTA, and the agent pipeline.

Recommended direction: make khoj.zuhaib.pro the polished public product site by either proxying/importing the KhojAI website experience or migrating the KhojAI app into the main Khoj repo. Keep lab.zuhaib.pro/khoj as the internal progress dashboard.
